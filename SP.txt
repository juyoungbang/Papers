1. Understanding Ephemeral Storage for Serverless Analytics (ATC '18)
Topic: Key insights to guide the design of an ephemeral cloud storage system
Main Idea: 1) The storage system should have high elasticity.
           2) The storage service should auto-scale resources based on load.
           3) The storage system can provide low data durability guarantees.
           4) Storing both small and large objects should be cost and performance efficient.
